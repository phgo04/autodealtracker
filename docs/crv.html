Used vs New — Which Should You Buy?

📊 Used vs New Analysis

Verdict: Used (2024–2025) is the smarter play right now.

Why used wins:

  • Price: Best used ($29–$37K) vs. best new ($41–$49K) = $12–$20K cheaper
  • Monthly cost: Used Sport at $37K = ~$580/mo vs. new Sport at $48K+ = ~$760/mo. Monthly gap is ~$180/mo—significant over 72 months
  • Depreciation: New cars lose 15–20% in year 1; used 2024 models have already absorbed that hit
  • Warranty: CPO Honda units come with manufacturer-backed 7yr/160k km coverage—nearly as good as new
  • Availability: 120+ used listings vs. ~8 new; vastly more choice in used market

When new becomes worth it:

  • If 2026 CR-V pricing drops below $40.5K (not currently happening)
  • If Mazda/Honda offers 0% financing on new (unlikely in current environment)
  • If you value brand-new warranty + zero mileage absolutely (cost = ~$2,000–$3,000 more; debatable ROI)

Best used strategy: Target 2024 Sport or LX-B models under $37K with under 35K km. Secure CPO if possible. You'll save $200–$300/mo vs. new while getting nearly identical condition.
